My Story

My drive doesn't come from a business book. It comes from watching my father.

He grew up in Albania during one of the hardest periods in its modern history — working from a young age, not for ambition, but for survival. To feed his siblings. To hold his family together. In 1993, shortly after the end of the dictatorship, he made his way to Santorini with almost nothing. He worked two, sometimes three jobs at a time. He didn't complain. He built.

Today, he runs a successful construction company on the island, working with some of Santorini's most prestigious hotels. He built that from zero.

Every time I see pride in his eyes when he looks at me, I feel something shift inside me. That look is fuel. It pushes me toward goals I once thought were out of reach.

I want to create value — real, tangible value — for businesses and for people. I believe the most powerful way to do that right now, at 18, is to build a startup. Not to get rich. To matter.

Hotel Email Automation Agent

Built with N8N

Hotels in Santorini receive hundreds of emails every day — from guests, from advertisers, from noise. I built a three-stage AI pipeline to handle it.

The first agent reads every incoming email and classifies it: is this an actual guest with a real request, or is it something that should be filtered out? If it's a guest, it passes the email to the second agent — the writer — who drafts a professional, context-aware reply. Before anything reaches the guest's inbox, a third agent reviews the drafted response and flags it as safe to send or not.

Three agents. One pipeline. No human needed in the middle.

Invoice Intelligence Dashboard

Vibe Coded + N8N

A mini SaaS tool I built entirely through vibe coding — with the exception of the AI analysis layer, which runs on N8N. Upload your invoices as PDFs, and let the system do the rest.

The AI agent extracts and analyzes each invoice, generates a short financial summary, and feeds everything into a clean dashboard. The platform organizes data by vendors and categories, visualizes spending patterns with charts, and turns what is usually a pile of paperwork into something actually useful — and readable.

Built to look and feel like a real product, because I think how something looks is part of how well it works.

Personal AI News Briefing
